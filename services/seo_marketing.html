SEO Marketing: Search Engine Optimization (SEO) / Search Marketing Services

If people can’t find you, they can’t do business with you.

Search marketing refers to the organic and paid search activities that help boost your website’s search engine visibility, drive visitors and generate leads.

It’s critical that your business gets found online when people are searching for your related products, services and knowledge. Why is Search Marketing an important part of our inbound marketing partnerships?

Why SEO?

  • 1.17 Billion people use search engines on a regular basis to find information online, according to Google.
  • 68% of search engine users click on listings that make the first page of results while only 8% go beyond the first three pages of results, according to a recent Jupiter Research study.
  • 39% of search engine users consider companies in the top natural search results to be leaders in their field, according to the Jupiter study.
  • Google was the chosen search engine for more than 72% of all U.S. Internet searches in March 2009, according to Hitwise.
